Government-Aligned Projects

Nai Manzil

Nai Manzil aims to engage constructively with poor Minority youth and help them obtain sustainable and gainful employment opportunities that can facilitate them to be integrated with mainstream economic activities.

Nai Roshni

"Nai Roshni" is the Scheme for Leadership Development of Minority Women, under the Ministry of Minority Affairs.

NULM (National Urban Livelihood Mission)

NULM is designed to provide skills to the unskilled urban poor as well as to upgrade their existing skills.

Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL)

RPL provides recognition to informal learning or learning through work to get equal acceptance as formal education.

Seekho Aur Kamao (Learn and Earn)

A 100% Central Sector Scheme for skill development of minorities, implemented by the Ministry of Minority Affairs since 2013‚Äì14.

USTTAD Scheme

Upgrading the Skills and Training in Traditional Arts/Crafts for Development under the Ministry of Minority Affairs.

Grant-in-Aid Welfare of Scheduled Tribes

This scheme enhances the reach of government welfare programs in tribal areas through voluntary efforts and organizations.

Ambedkar Hastshilp Vikas Yojana

This scheme promotes premium handicrafts, supports mass production, and preserves heritage crafts through development commissions.

Hunar Se Rozgar Tak (HSRT)

HSRT creates employable skills amongst youth in the hospitality and tourism sector.
